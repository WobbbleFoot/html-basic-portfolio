My Favourite Movies by WobbbleFoot

Top 5 Movies

Oppenheimer

One of the best Cinematic Non-Fiction Storytelling movies of all time; No we aren't arguing about this.

The Death of Stalin

Really funny and surprisingly accurate! Caught me by surprise the first time I watched it. Also I love the accents.

Schindler's List

A heart touching film that deeply moves me everytime I watch it. Shows the grotesqueness of it all in Nazi Germany.

All Quiet On The Western Front

A film, unlike others, that does not glorify wars and shows the horror of it all.

American Psycho

A cult classic. Reminds me to live life for myself and not for others.
